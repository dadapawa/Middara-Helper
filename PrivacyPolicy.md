# Privacy Policy for Middara Helper

**Last Updated:** February 9, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Middara Helper handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information Collection and Storage

**Middara Helper does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers or the internet.**

All information you enter into the App is stored locally on your device only. This includes:
- Game progress and saved data
- Player status and items
- User preferences and settings
- Any other information you input into the App

## Data Transmission

**Local Network Sharing:**
The App includes a feature that allows you to share data with other devices running Middara Helper on the same WiFi network. When you use this feature:
- Player status and items are transmitted between connected devices
- Data transmission occurs only on your local network (WiFi)
- No data is sent to external servers or over the internet

**Internet Transmission:**
The App does not transmit any data over the internet or to external servers. All functionality operates without requiring an internet connection.

## Third-Party Services

Middara Helper does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device or is shared only on your local network, the security of your information depends on:
- Your device's security settings
- Your device's physical security
- Your WiFi network's security (when using the local network sharing feature)
- Any backup services you may use (e.g., Google Drive, device backups)

We recommend:
- Keeping your device secure with appropriate lock screen protection
- Using a secure, password-protected WiFi network
- Only sharing data with trusted devices on your local network

## Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. To delete all App data:
- Uninstall the App from your device, or
- Clear the App's data through your device's application settings

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
